A warm welcome awaits...

We are a family run equestrian centre, based in Ashby, Leicestershire. Our aim is to provide great quality lessons within an enjoyable and supportive environment and we pride ourselves on our high standard of horse care.

We offer a large indoor school and smaller outdoor school where private, semi-private or group lessons can be held. In addition we have 200 acres of land suitable for off road hacking.

About Oak Tree Riding School


Founded in 1995 we have a wealth of knowledge, our facilities include:

  • A large indoor school (60 x 30m)
  • A smaller outdoor school
  • A cross country course
  • An indoor classroom for theory lessons

Our Instructors
Liz Harding
Liz is the owner and senior instructor at Oak Tree Riding School. Her passion is for dressage, where she has competed up to Grand Prix level. She loves to see new students at the school progress as well as help existing equestrians excel.
Zoe Wicks
Zoe has been teaching at Oak Tree Riding School for the past four years. She excels at teaching beginners and takes alot of our beginner group classes and hacks. She has her own horses and competes locally.
Georgia Grey
Georgia is our advanced jumping instructor. She has competed locally since childhood in show jumping and team chasing ! She enjoys getting students confidence up in both show jumping and cross country.
